§02

Two emergencies.
One valve.

Most valves do one job. California Smart Valve handles both — in a single device, on a single install.

Threat 01 — Seismic

When the ground moves,
the gas stops.

Internal seismic sensors trigger an immediate shutoff during earthquakes — closing the gas line before a ruptured pipe can find a spark.

  • Solid-state seismic detection
  • Instant mechanical shutoff
  • Manual reset after the all-clear
Threat 02 — Fire

When the heat rises,
the gas stops.

A fire-emergency response system shuts off gas supply when temperatures spike — cutting fuel from the fire before it reaches the line.

  • Thermal trigger built in
  • Closed-loop electronic shutoff
  • No external power required

FIRST OF ITS KIND First valve to combine seismic detection and fire response in a single, easy-to-install device.
